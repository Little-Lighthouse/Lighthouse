Warmly welcome to our Little Lighthouse, where knowledge blossoms and inspiration flourishes!

Founded on 13th July 2023

Ran as Youth Org since January 2024


Little Lighthouse (LLH) is a youth organization mainly focuses on content creation, which involves sharing knowledge and essential basic skills in life for young minds to be happy in life.

At Little Lighthouse, our mission is to focus on the happiness of youth by fostering personal development.

About Little Lighthouse

As the name suggests, "Little Lighthouse," our dedicated goal is to illuminate your path and guide you through obstacles and challenges with unwavering commitment. We're not just about guiding you through challenges and obstacles. We are committed to expanding our reach and making a positive impact on society.
